How are Kotak PoS charge-back cases defended?

When a cardholder, remitter, or customer uses a PoS machine to conduct a UPI or card transaction, the amount is debited from their account. However, the office does not provide any service (e.g., neither issuing a policy nor providing a money receipt). Consequently, Kotak Bank receives a complaint or dispute from the cardholder or the cardholder's bank regarding the card/UPI transaction processed at your office. To address this dispute, please provide the following documents for investigation and remediation within 3 calendar days from the date of this communication.
  1. Provide a copy of the policy if it has already been issued for the said transaction.
  2. Provide a copy of the money receipt if payment has been received but the policy has not been issued for the said transaction.
  3. If, upon reviewing your records, you find that the cardholder's complaint is justified, please instruct the bank to reverse the transaction amount back to the cardholder's bank.
  4. Please note, in the event of irrelevant, inadequate or no response being received within the stipulated timeframe (within 03 calendar days from the date of this communication) or in case incomplete documentation is received, bank would be constrained to debit UIIC account, or a future settlement towards recovery for the said transaction. Please acknowledge the receipt of this communication.

What steps should be taken to address an unidentified transaction?

  2. Unidentified transaction can be returned to the source account, if the transaction is initiated from your PoS machine however it is not identified by offices.
